01 — The Mechanic

One target, split into pools that fill in real time.

Every region-developer combination is its own revenue pool. As Sales Executives close bookings, the pool fills — and once it's full, the reward pool tied to it unlocks for everyone who contributed.

Pool

Region × Developer

Each city and developer combination — Godrej Pune, VTP Mumbai, Lodha Thane — runs its own live target and reward pool.

Team

Rolled up by Manager

Individual contributions roll up into team totals, so every Manager can see exactly where their squad stands against the Grand Prize line.

You

Your own tracker

Search your name to see your share of every pool you've contributed to — and how close you are to a legendary solo run.

02 — The Payoff

Three ways to win.

Rewards stack — a single quarter can earn a team the Grand Prize, a pool bonus, and an individual legendary reward all at once.

Pool Level

Target Hit Bonus

The moment a region-developer pool crosses 100%, its reward pool splits across every contributor by revenue share.

Individual · Rare

The 50% Rule

Close more than half of a pool's revenue solo, and it's a legendary run — flagged on the leaderboard with its own badge and bonus.

Team Level

Grand Prize

Any Manager's team that crosses ₹75 Cr in combined revenue for the quarter qualifies for the company-wide Grand Prize.
